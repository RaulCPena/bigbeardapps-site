Features

Everything your recipe collection needs

Share a video or post from any app and Feastmark extracts the recipe automatically — ingredients, instructions, and all.

⚡️

Save in One Tap

Share any recipe video or post to Feastmark. We extract the full recipe automatically — even when it's only spoken in the video.

👨‍🍳

Cook Mode

A hands-free cooking view: check off ingredients, swipe through steps in big type, and the screen stays awake until you're done.

✏️

Make It Yours

Edit any recipe, add personal notes, or write one from scratch with your own photo. Your cookbook, your rules.

📚

Collections

Organize recipes into collections like "Weeknight Dinners" or "Date Night." Filter your library in seconds.

⚖️

Ingredient Scaling

Cooking for two or twelve? Adjust serving size and every ingredient scales automatically.

📎

Add from Anywhere

No share sheet handy? Paste a link right in the app. Can't extract it? Save it as a draft and finish it later.

🔍

Search Everything

Search by name, tag, or ingredient — "what can I make with chicken?" — and find exactly what you're craving.

🔒

Private by Design

Your recipes stay on your device. No account required, no data sold. Ever.
