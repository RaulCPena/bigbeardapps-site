Scenario Simulator

Test "what if" scenarios with extra payments or windfalls. See exactly how much time and money you'll save.

Smart Reminders

Never miss a payment with automatic reminders before due dates. Avoid late fees and stay on track.

PDF Export

Export professional payment schedules and debt summaries to share with advisors or save for reference.

Beautiful Design

Native iOS app built in SwiftUI with full dark mode support, colorful charts, and intuitive navigation.

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's encrypted SwiftData framework. No account required, no data collection, no tracking.
