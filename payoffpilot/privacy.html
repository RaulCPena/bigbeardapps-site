Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 9, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: PayoffPilot is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, track, store, or share any of your personal or financial information. Your data is yours alone.

Information We Collect

Short answer: None.

PayoffPilot does not collect any personal information, financial data, or usage analytics. The app operates entirely on your device without any data transmission to external servers.

Data Storage

All your financial data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Debt information (balances, interest rates, payment history)
  • Personal financial profile (income, budget preferences)
  • Payment schedules and calculations
  • App preferences and settings

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to use iCloud sync (see below).

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync on your device:

  • Your data is encrypted end-to-end before transmission
  • Data is synced across your personal Apple devices only
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • You control iCloud sync through iOS Settings
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time

What We Don't Do

PayoffPilot explicitly does NOT:

  • ❌ Collect any personal information
  • ❌ Track your location
  • ❌ Use analytics or tracking tools
  • ❌ Share data with third parties
  • ❌ Use advertising networks
  • ❌ Require account creation
  • ❌ Access your contacts or photos
  • ❌ Connect to social media

Local Notifications

If you enable payment due date reminders:

  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device
  • No data is sent to any server
  • You can disable notifications anytime in iOS Settings
  • Notifications may include debt names and payment amounts from your local data

PDF Exports

When you export payment schedules or debt summaries as PDF:

  • PDFs are generated locally on your device
  • No data is uploaded to any server
  • You control where PDFs are shared (email, AirDrop, Files, etc.)
  • We have no access to exported files

Data Security

Your data is protected through:

  • Local encryption at rest using iOS security features
  • No network transmission of sensitive data
  • Face ID/Touch ID/Passcode protection (system level)
  • Secure deletion when you reset app data

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • ✅ Access: View all your data anytime within the app
  • ✅ Export: Export payment schedules and summaries as PDF
  • ✅ Delete: Permanently delete all data through Settings
  • ✅ Control: No data leaves your device without your explicit action

Third-Party Services

None. PayoffPilot does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data processors.

Children's Privacy

PayoffPilot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for adults managing personal debt.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists as long as the app is installed
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can manually delete all data through app Settings
  • We retain no data on any servers

Legal Compliance

PayoffPilot complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Apple App Store Privacy Requirements

Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to comply with - you're automatically protected.
