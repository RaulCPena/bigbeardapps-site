Studio boilerplate — short

Big Beard Apps is an independent one-person iOS studio building privacy-first apps with no ads, no tracking, and no data collection.

Studio boilerplate — long

Big Beard Apps is an independent one-person iOS studio. Its apps are built around a simple position: your data belongs on your device. Nothing requires an account, nothing is sold to third parties, and no app carries ads or trackers. The studio's first two apps are Feastmark, which turns recipe videos into a personal cookbook, and PayoffPilot, a private debt-payoff planner sold as a one-time purchase rather than a subscription.

Descriptions

One line

Feastmark saves recipes from Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube in one tap — even when the recipe is only spoken in the video.

Short — about 50 words

Feastmark turns recipe videos into a cookbook you'll actually use. Share any post from Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube and the full recipe is extracted automatically — including recipes that are only spoken aloud. A hands-free Cook Mode keeps the screen awake while you cook. Everything stays on your device.

Long — about 100 words

Most people save hundreds of recipe videos and cook almost none of them, because a video is a terrible cookbook. Feastmark fixes that: share any recipe post from Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube and it extracts the full ingredient list and steps automatically — even when the recipe is never written down and exists only in the creator's narration. From there it's yours to edit, annotate, scale to any serving size, sort into collections, and search by ingredient. Cook Mode gives you a checklist and big-type steps with the screen kept awake. No account, no tracking, and recipes never leave your phone.

What makes it different

  • It hears recipes. When a creator never writes the recipe down, Feastmark works from the video's spoken audio — the feature most recipe savers don't have.
  • One tap from the share sheet. No copy-pasting links between apps.
  • Cook Mode. Ingredient checklist, swipeable big-type steps, screen stays awake — built for hands covered in flour.
  • Private by design. No account, no data collection; your library lives on your device.

Descriptions

One line

PayoffPilot plans your way out of debt — Snowball vs. Avalanche on your real numbers, with no subscription and no bank linking.

Short — about 50 words

PayoffPilot is a private debt-payoff planner. Compare the Snowball and Avalanche strategies side by side on your real balances, see a calendar date for going debt-free, and simulate what an extra $200 a month would do. No account, no bank linking, and Pro is a one-time purchase.

Long — about 100 words

People paying off debt do not need another monthly bill. PayoffPilot is built on that premise: it is free to use, its Pro tier is a single one-time purchase rather than a subscription, and it never asks to connect to a bank. You enter your debts, and it shows the Snowball and Avalanche strategies side by side — interest saved, time saved, which debt to attack first — plus a real calendar date for being debt-free that updates with every payment you log. A scenario simulator tests extra payments before you commit real money. Everything stays on your device, optionally behind Face ID.

What makes it different

  • One-time purchase, not a subscription. The people this app serves are trying to cut recurring bills, not add one.
  • No bank linking, ever. No credentials, no aggregator, no account — the numbers are typed in and stay put.
  • Both strategies, honestly compared. Snowball and Avalanche side by side on real balances, not a generic explainer.
  • Fully offline and lockable. Works with no connection; optional Face ID or Touch ID lock.
